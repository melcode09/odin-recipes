Ingredients

  • Salmom
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Marinade the salmon with salt, pepper, and lemon.
  3. Melt the butter in a microwave and baste salmon with it.
  4. Put salmon in the oven for 20 - 25 minutes.
